Working Papers

Work from Home, Internal Migration and Gender Wage Gap in Latin America
with Jeanne Lafortune and Hugo Silva
This paper examines the effects of teleworking on internal migration and gender wage gaps in Latin America, using household survey data from Chile, Peru, Mexico, Colombia, and Brazil. We compare outcomes before and after the pandemic and find that teleworking is more common among urban residents and may reduce gender wage gaps and migration pressures.
